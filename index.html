About us

SWEED Trust (Social Women Educational and Economical Development Trust) is a service-minded voluntary and non-governmental organization established in 2014 and registered in the sub-registrar office in Thiruparankundram with Reg. No: 15/2014 under the Indian Trust Act 1882. Our mission is to address ignorance, illiteracy, and exploitation, which are major hindrances for socially deprived and disadvantaged people, and to improve their standard of life through various support programs.

We strive to empower women and children by providing education, vocational training, and access to healthcare. By fostering a sense of community and self-reliance, we aim to create sustainable development and long-term social change. Our holistic approach ensures that all aspects of an individual's well-being are addressed, promoting a more inclusive and equitable society. Join us in our mission to uplift the underprivileged and make a lasting impact on their lives.

Achievements

We are proud to highlight the following achievements:

  • Formation of 2000 Women's Self-Help Groups (SHGs)
  • Orientation training provided to 15,000 SHG members
  • Assistance in obtaining 25 Aadhar Cards and 50 old age pensions
  • Skill training given to 13,000 individuals in tailoring and computer skills
  • Manufacturing of household cleaning products by 1500 people
  • Organizing 300 awareness programmes
  • Sheltering 42 inmates in the NULM Shelter Home

    • Programmes

    Each year, SWEED Trust conducts a variety of programmes in line with the objectives outlined in our trust deed and the National Livelihood Mission (Govt. of India). These programmes are designed to address the basic needs and promote the overall well-being of disadvantaged communities.

    We focus on providing comprehensive support, including educational initiatives, skill development workshops, and healthcare services. Our efforts also extend to environmental sustainability projects and community-building activities. Through these diverse programs, we aim to foster economic independence and improve the quality of life for those we serve.

    Additionally, SWEED Trust collaborates with local and national organizations to amplify our impact and reach more beneficiaries.
