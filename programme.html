Programmes

Each year, SWEED Trust conducts a variety of programmes in line with the objectives outlined in our trust deed and the National Livelihood Mission (Govt. of India). These programmes are designed to address the basic needs and promote the overall well-being of disadvantaged communities.

We focus on providing comprehensive support, including educational initiatives, skill development workshops, and healthcare services. Our efforts also extend to environmental sustainability projects and community-building activities. Through these diverse programs, we aim to foster economic independence and improve the quality of life for those we serve.

Comprehensive Community Support Initiatives

Each year, SWEED Trust conducts a variety of programmes in line with the objectives outlined in our trust deed and the National Livelihood Mission (Govt. of India). These programmes are designed to address the basic needs and promote the overall well-being of disadvantaged communities.

  1. Food: Regular distribution of nutritious meals to ensure no one goes hungry.
  2. Clothing: Collection and distribution of clothing to those in need.
  3. Shelter: Providing safe and secure housing for homeless individuals and families.
  4. Medical Check-ups: Regular health screenings and medical care to monitor and maintain the health of our beneficiaries.
  5. Festival Celebrations: Organizing events and celebrations for various cultural and religious festivals to foster a sense of community and joy among the people we serve.

Empowering Communities

Transforming Lives Through Comprehensive Support

Comprehensive Community Support Initiatives

In addition to these basic services, we focus on skill development training to empower individuals with the tools they need for economic independence. Our training programs include:

Tailoring Courses

We help individuals, especially women, gain skills for self-employment through our tailoring courses.

Computer Courses

Enhancing digital literacy and improving employment opportunities with our comprehensive computer courses.

Household Cleaning Products Workshops

Participants learn to manufacture household cleaning products, enabling them to start small businesses.

Access to Welfare Schemes

Our team assists people in obtaining Aadhar cards, voter IDs, and old age pensions, ensuring they receive the support they are entitled to.

Awareness Programs

We organize campaigns and workshops to educate the community on health and hygiene, women's rights,child protection, and environmental sustainability.
